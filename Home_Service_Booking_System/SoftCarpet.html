Soft Carpet Cleaning Service At RJ Home Services, we believe that clean carpets make for a cozy and inviting home. Our Soft Carpet Cleaning Service is tailored to gently but effectively lift dirt, dust, and allergens from your carpets, leaving them fresh, soft, and revitalized.

Pricing: 500.00 Rs/Sq

  • Pre-treatment: We start by applying an eco-friendly, non-toxic pre-treatment solution to loosen embedded dirt and stains.
  • Deep Cleaning: Using advanced equipment, we gently lift dirt and allergens from deep within the carpet fibers, preserving softness and color.
  • Stain Removal: Specialized treatments are used to tackle tough stains, ensuring they are safely removed without damaging the carpet.
  • Fresh Finishing: We finish with a gentle deodorizing and sanitizing process, leaving your carpets fresh, clean, and soft underfoot.
